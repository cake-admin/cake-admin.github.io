Lenovo’s AI identity will maintain consistency across all software applications. The Moto AI logo has been adapted as the standard iconography for AI features within these applications.

Components

Smart, ready-to-use patterns for common AI interactions—like disclaimers, loading states, and helpful button layouts. Designed to guide users, build trust, and make working with AI feel intuitive from start to finish.
