Accordion

Developers - Detailed specifications for this component are avalible in the Accordion section in Figma.

The accordion component is designed to efficiently manage and display content in a collapsible and expandable format. It allows users to interact with sections of content, revealing or hiding information as needed, thereby enhancing user experience and optimizing space utilization.
